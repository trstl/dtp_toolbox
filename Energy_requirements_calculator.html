Energy Requirements Calculator

Harris-Benedict, Mifflin-St Jeor, Penn State, and Toronto equations

Adjust the weight, height, and age values according to your values. The output will be displayed below the input section after calculations.

**TBSA** represents the total burn surface area expressed in % (0-100 range). **CI** represents the ingested calories expressed in kcal. **days** represents days after burn incident. **Activity factor** will be used to multiply the estimated requirements. **TBSA Stress factors** can be left blank or adjusted manually. If left blank, the calculator will choose a stress factor based on TBSA.
